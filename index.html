Profit Sharing & Terms

Structured to Align Our Interests with Yours

Our profit-sharing structure is straightforward and transparent, designed so that Arambhi Investments only succeeds when our partners do.

Profit Distribution
80% of net profits are distributed to investors. The remaining 20% goes to Arambhi Investments for management and operational oversight.

Risk Disclosures

Important Risks to Understand

Risk 01
No Guarantee of Returns
Target returns are projections based on current assumptions and internal modeling. Actual results may differ materially. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
Risk 02
Risk of Loss
Real estate investments are speculative and involve significant risk, including the potential loss of your entire investment. Only invest capital you can afford to lose.
Risk 03
Illiquidity
Investments are generally illiquid. There is no secondary market for these interests. Investors must be prepared to hold for the full project term, typically 3–7 years.
Risk 04
Regulatory & Market Risk
Zoning laws, tax policies, interest rates, and environmental regulations may affect property values and operational requirements over the investment period.
